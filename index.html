Events and Works

A list of all the events organized by the Students' Forum for Mathematics, DMU along with the Department of Mathematics, DMU

25/08/2024

The forum organized a one-day social service program on the 25th August, 2024.

13/05/2024

A new Convenor-in-Charge was selected on 13th May, 2024. Two new Co-Convenor-in-Charges were also selected for DM Science and DM Arts.

×

06/05/2024

The forum organized a fund drive to help the students affected by the hailstorm that tore down thousands of houses on 5th May, 2024

29/02/2024

After more than 4 months of construction, the forum, along with the Department of Mathematics, DM Science, inaugurated the Study Park of the Dept. of Mathematics, DM Science.

×

08/01/2024

A general meeting was held at the central hall of DM College of Science for a significant expansion of the members of the forum, which was the inclusion of the students of DM Arts and GP Women's College.

22/12/2023

Organized by the forum, for the first time ever in DMU, National Mathematics Day was celebrated on the 22nd December 2023 at the Central Hall of DM Science

×

14/10/2023

The forum, along with the Department of Mathematics, DM Science, organized a one day social service cum tree plantation program.

×

09/10/2024

The forum lodged a complaint to the VC of DMU for the scarcity of teachers in the Mathematics Department of DM Science.

26/09/2023

A General Body meeting was held at the PG Block, Dept. of Mathematics, DMU in the presence of 250 students of Mathematics Dept. of DM Science and the Students' Forum for Mathematics was formed.

About Us

The Students' Forum for Mathematics, DMU, is dedicated to fostering a supportive academic environment. We organize events to bring students together and ensure a neat and clean atmosphere conducive to learning. Our goal is to listen to students' complaints and work towards resolving them efficiently.
